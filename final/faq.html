What is an immersive sim?

"Immersive Sim" (immersive simulation) is a term used to describe some games. The definition is hotly debated. I make no definitive claims here but I do offer a feel for what most people agree on. An immersive sim includes elements of Systemic Design. For instance:

All wood catches on fire, and all fire ignites wood.

When the game is built with such rules in place and the player given an objective, possibilities for player creativity to solve puzzles and overcome obstacles in novel ways show up.

This might show up in an example of opening a door with explosives. A traditional game might ask you to collect and place a specific explosive at a specific place on the wall before the game will allow the door to be destroyed.
Even if you have grenades or a rocket launcher that would do the trick in the real world, the door won't budge.

In an immersive sim with explosives and destructable doors, any explosive you can think of should do the trick. That is the appeal of the Immersive sim.
