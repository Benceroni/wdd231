Site Plan

Site Name

CompulsiveSimStudio.us

Site purpose

Providing information about my efforts in game development, links to my youtube channel and answer frequently asked questions.

Scenarios

I : I'm having a conversation with someone. He or she asks what I like to do outside of work or the like and, as a professional programmer, I have very little desire or ability to converse with him or her, so I offer them a business card with a link to this website which explains my game development, offers link and a well thought out presentation to the uninitiateds

II : I'm having a rich conversation about my game development with someone who isn't "in the know" and he or she asks what genre my game is. Their follow up question, "what is an immersive sim" is another thing I seek to answer with my website.
